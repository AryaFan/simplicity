You can use ```img``` shortcode to insert image with lightbox.

Example code:

```html
&#123;{< img src="/images/image-1.jpg" title="Optional title" >}&#125;
```

Result (click on the image):

{{< img src="/images/image-1.jpg" title="Optional title" >}}
